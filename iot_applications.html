Smart Building

What does smart mean?
Smart grammatically means well organised but in other scenario it refers to intelligence.
Smart means doing the work with more clear mind to get some ideas to work fastly and sharply to minimize the time and effort required for the same task.

what are some building scenario?
A Smart building scenario is that it have to interact with with people, system and the external environment/ surrounding.
Building have to adapt according to the need of people. Mainly it refers to providing more safety, security and comfort which increase the efficiency of work.

what are the main features needed?

Activity3

Control Interface

What are the high level features?
The control interface is bidirectional and processes information to and from the user and provides option to interact with different devices.
Control interface reflects the device that is being controlled and the function of the assistive technology.
Mostly people use different interfaces to access the same device, but for different action.

What are the parts needed?
Sensors
Actuators
Data as an input and output
Buttons
Navigation
Strong network and alternatives

Who are the users
Consumers of the interface are from varying age groups, depending on the in-built features it. For example, in the case of smart watches, the majority of users are in the 20-40 age group, with a few users in the 12-20 and 40-60 age groups.
Moreover, the Consumers mainly includes the employee, staff or the general public.
